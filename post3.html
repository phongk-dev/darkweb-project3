Dark Web Marketplaces

Dark web marketplaces operate in hidden corners of the internet, offering a variety of illicit goods and services. These platforms often use cryptocurrencies to facilitate anonymous transactions, making it difficult for authorities to track buyers and sellers.

Some of the most infamous dark web markets have been shut down by law enforcement, but new ones continue to emerge. Users often rely on invitation-only forums and encrypted messaging platforms to share marketplace links and discuss trusted vendors.

Despite their anonymity, many dark web markets have implemented rating systems similar to mainstream e-commerce platforms. This allows users to review sellers based on product quality, delivery speed, and reliability.
